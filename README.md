## Thoughts

`Thoughts` is a place to write, publish, and share your ideas with the world. It is designed for writers and readers who want a clean, focused experience without distractions.

### Key features

- **Beautiful writing experience**: Minimal, distraction-free editor focused on your words.
- **Publishing workflow**: Draft, preview, and publish posts in a few simple steps.
- **Reader-friendly layouts**: Responsive layouts that look great on desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **Profiles and discovery**: Create a profile, share your work, and discover other writers.

### Project structure

- **`apps/web`**: Main web application for reading and writing posts.
- **`apps/backend`**: TRPC backend serving the client requests.
